About dishmo

Dishmo was created to build a better internet restaurant menu. The days of plain menus with no interactivity are gone.

So, say goodbye to plate envy. Dishmo is the app that answers the question, "What’s good here?"

Have you ever been to an expensive restaurant and regretted what you ordered? Have you ever received your food and it looked nothing like what you were expecting?

With Dishmo, users can view complete menus of local eateries and get the dish on what to order. Individual menu items are reviewed by customers and images of the actual dish can be uploaded to the review pages. Reviews are shareable on social media so that you can let your friends know about your restaurant experiences; good or bad.

With options to sort restaurants by neighborhood, price, rating, cuisine type, and special diet, Dishmo helps you find the best dish to please your palate.

Dishmo is also useful for restaurants. Restaurants have the ability to receive specific data on every menu item in their restaurant. Are customers not liking the sauce? Do they hate the sides that come with a specific dish? Are the dishes coming out and looking like they should?

Dishmo helps answer all these questions with real feedback from real customers. To get your restaurant on our site, contact us here: Contact Us
