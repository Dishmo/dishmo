About dishmo

Your online restaurant menu should elevate your establishment's offerings, not distract from them.

At dishmo, we set out to build a better restaurant menu. With our sleek, straightforward designs, guests will be able to effortlessly peruse menu items in a mobile-responsive format. No more trapping your menu in a clunky PDF. It's time to let your dishes shine.

dishmo menus are made to be useful for restaurants. Restaurants have the ability to receive specific data on every menu item in their restaurant. Are customers not liking the sauce? Do they hate the sides that come with a meal?

dishmo helps answer all these questions with real feedback from real customers. Using Facebook comments, users can share their experiences at your restaurant directly with you, and with their friends on Facebook.
